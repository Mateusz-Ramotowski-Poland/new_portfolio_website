About

I was born and raised in Masuria. Thanks to my strict mind, in Gdańsk, I obtained the title of electronics technician and a master's degree in automation and robotics.

I wrote my first computer program in 2013 programming PLC controller. During my studies, I learned the basics of several programming languages: Python, C, C++, PHP, JS, HTML, CSS, Matlab and used MySQL and MongoDB databases. My 2 diploma theses consisted in creating and describing the operation of computer programs.

However, after graduating in November 2019, I wanted to work in a different technical profession than a programmer. For 2 years I worked in 3 different companies as an electrician, wireman and automation engineer. In October 2021, I decided that I want to work as a programmer. Since then, I've been learning to create better applications and websites.

In the graphic, you can see what technologies I have been using since October 2021. I like creating websites. It gives me joy to see a good, responsive website that I created after many hours of writing and debugging the code.

In my free time, I like physical activity. I run a lot - I am preparing for a half-marathon, and I swim regularly. I like hiking in the mountains - in May 2021 I was in the Devero Alps in Italy. I also love strategy board games - Kaverna, Cyclades, 7 Wonders, Warhammer Invasion....
